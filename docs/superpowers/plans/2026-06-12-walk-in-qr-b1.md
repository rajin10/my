# Walk-in QR — B1 (online) Implementation Plan

> **For agentic workers:** REQUIRED SUB-SKILL: Use superpowers:subagent-driven-development (recommended) or superpowers:executing-plans to implement this plan task-by-task. Steps use checkbox (`- [ ]`) syntax for tracking.

**Goal:** Ship online walk-in QR booking/order for guests and signed-in customers (no LAN yet).

**Architecture:** Schema adds guest + walk-in fields; new `/api/v1/walk-in` module handles context, submit, branch QR, sessions; mobile/owner apps add QR scan/display and fast checkout screens wired to API.

**Tech Stack:** Drizzle/D1, Hono, `@repo/api-client`, Expo 56 (`expo-camera`, `react-native-qrcode-svg`)

**Spec:** [2026-06-12-walk-in-qr-lan-sync-design.md](../specs/2026-06-12-walk-in-qr-lan-sync-design.md)
