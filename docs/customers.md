# Customer Guide

Everything you need to discover, book, and manage services on Talash.

Talash is available on the **web** ([marketing site](https://talash.bd)) and the **mobile app** (Google Play). Flows are the same on both — search, book, manage appointments, save favourites, and earn rewards.

---

## Getting started

### Sign in with Google

Talash uses **Google sign-in only** — there is no separate password or SMS code to remember.

**Web:** tap **Sign in**, choose your Google account, and you are returned to Talash signed in.

**Mobile app:** tap **Sign in with Google** and complete sign-in in the in-app browser (or the native Google picker in the installed app).

Your session stays active on the same device until you sign out.

---

## Discovering businesses

### Searching for services

Use search to find what you need. You can filter by:

- **Service or business name**
- **City or area**
- **Category** (salon, spa, massage, wellness, and more)
- **Price range**, **minimum rating**, and **sort order**

### What you'll see in the results

Each business card shows the name, location, rating, price range, and a cover photo when available. Tap a business for the full profile — branches, services, photos, opening hours, and reviews.

---

## Booking a service

### Step by step

1. **Open a business** from search or favourites.
2. **Choose a branch** if the business has more than one location.
3. **Select a service** — duration and price are shown for each.
4. **Pick a date and time** from available slots (based on branch hours and existing bookings).
5. **Optional:** apply a **coupon code** at checkout if the business gave you one.
6. **Confirm** — payment is **pay at business**; no card charge in the app today.

### What happens next

Your booking starts as **Pending** while the business reviews it. When they confirm, it becomes **Confirmed**. After your appointment, the business marks it **Completed**.

---

## Managing your bookings

### Viewing appointments

Open **Bookings** (app) or **Account → My bookings** (web). Each row shows business, service, date, time, and status: **Pending**, **Confirmed**, **Cancelled**, or **Completed**.

### Cancelling

You can cancel **Pending** or **Confirmed** bookings from the booking detail view. The slot is released and the business is notified.

---

## Saving favourites

Tap the heart on a business to save it. All favourites are in the **Favourites** tab (synced to your account on every device when signed in).

---

## Leaving a review

Reviews are available on **Completed** bookings only:

1. Open the booking.
2. Tap **Leave a review**.
3. Choose a star rating (1–5) and optional comment.
4. Submit — the business approves reviews before they appear on their public profile.

---

## Loyalty rewards

You earn points when businesses confirm your bookings. Open **Rewards** to see your balance and transaction history.

To redeem: tap **Redeem points**, enter how many to use, and confirm. You need enough balance for the amount you request.

---

## Using discount coupons

At checkout (web or app):

1. Open **Have a coupon?** and enter the code.
2. Tap **Apply** — the discount appears in the summary before you confirm.
3. Complete the booking with the discounted total.

Coupons are issued per business and only work for that business's services.

---

## Your account

### Profile and sessions

**Account** shows your profile. You can sign out, view active sessions, and revoke a session on another device.

### Notifications

Push and in-app notifications cover booking confirmations, cancellations, and other updates when you use the mobile app.

### Getting help

Contact the business from your booking details for appointment questions. For platform issues, use in-app support options when available.
