# Business Owner & Manager Guide

Everything you need to set up your business on Talash, manage bookings, and grow your client base.

Use the **business dashboard** (web) or the **owner app** (mobile). Both connect to the same business data.

---

## Getting started

### Sign in with Google

Sign in with your **Google account** — the same method customers use. There is no separate Talash password.

**Web dashboard:** open the studio URL and sign in with Google.

**Owner app:** tap **Sign in with Google**.

### First-time setup

If you have no business yet, you are guided through three steps:

1. **Create your business** — name, category, description.
2. **Add your first branch** — address, city, phone.
3. **Add your first service** — name, duration, price.

The same sequence applies on web (**Onboarding**) and mobile (**Setup**). After that you land on your dashboard.

### Understanding roles

| Role | What they can do |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Full control — business, team, bookings, settings, archive business |
| **Manager** | Bookings, services, staff, branches, coupons, campaigns |
| **Staff** | View schedule; can be assigned to bookings |
| **Customer** | Book services, leave reviews (separate customer apps) |

You are the **Owner** when you create a business. Invite team members and assign roles from **Team**.

---

## Setting up your business

### Business profile

Add your business name, description, category, and contact details (phone, email, website). New businesses start in **Draft** — not visible in customer search.

### Going live

Change status to **Active** when you are ready for bookings. Customers can then find you in search.

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Draft** | Setup in progress — hidden from search |
| **Active** | Live and bookable |
| **Suspended** | Temporarily paused — can return to Active |

Allowed transitions: Draft → Active, Active → Suspended, Suspended → Active.

To hide the business permanently, use **Archive** (soft-delete); you can **Restore** from settings if needed.

---

## Branches and hours

Add a branch per location (name, address, city, phone, optional map coordinates).

Set **working hours** per branch (open/close per day, or closed). Booking slots are only offered inside those hours.

---

## Services

Each service belongs to a branch: name, description, duration, price, category. You can upload a **photo** per service. Edit or remove services anytime; existing bookings are not deleted.

---

## Managing bookings

### Workflow

1. Customer books → **Pending** in your list.
2. **Confirm** to accept (customer is notified; loyalty points are credited).
3. Optionally **assign staff** to the booking.
4. After the appointment, **Mark complete**.

You can **Cancel** from Pending or Confirmed if needed.

### Views

- **Bookings list** — filter by status (including Completed).
- **Calendar** — day view; tap a card for full actions (mobile).
- **Export** — download bookings as CSV (web and owner app).

---

## Team

Add members by searching for an existing Talash user (email/name). Set role, title, and branch.

**Staff availability:** set each member's weekly schedule (owner/manager) so you know when they work.

---

## Photos, coupons, campaigns

- **Photos** — upload, reorder, and delete business gallery images.
- **Coupons** — percentage or fixed discount, usage limits, start/expiry dates; customers apply codes at checkout.
- **Campaigns** — draft and send marketing messages to customer segments.

---

## Reviews and analytics

- **Reviews** — approve or reject customer reviews before they go public.
- **Analytics** — overview, revenue, top services, peak hours, review stats, coupon performance, and staff metrics (7 / 30 / 90 day ranges).

---

## Notifications

In-app notifications cover new bookings, cancellations, and review submissions. Push tokens are registered on mobile owner devices.

---

## Getting help

Use in-app help where available. For booking issues, contact the customer from the booking detail view.
