## What is Talash?

Talash connects people who need services with businesses that provide them — salons, spas, fitness studios, clinics, and more. You can search for what you need, book an appointment in seconds, and manage everything from your phone or browser.

If you run a business, Talash gives you the tools to manage your locations, services, team, bookings, and customer relationships all in one place.
